Donkey is a stubborn animal that likes to joke around. Donkey stole Friendly Snowman nose for fun and because he wanted to eat it. Donkey can give you Snowman nose, the carrot back if you kill the Orc that keeps attacking him and bring back its blood. Carrot in exchange for Orc blood. Donkey is lonely and he dreams of adventures. Donkey wants to travel and go on an adventure with you. To go with you on an adventure, Donkey must fit into your backpack. Donkey is too big to fit into the backpack. Dark Elf can make Donkey smaller so he can fit into the backpack. Dark Elf lives in the Dreary Forest.
