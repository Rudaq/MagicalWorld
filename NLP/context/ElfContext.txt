Elf is a magical creature living in the Enchanted Forest. Elf is close to nature and other creatures. Elf can tell you how to save Snowman life. Ice Monster haunts on Friendly Snowman. To save Friendly Snowman life you need to kill the Ice Monster. After you kill the orc, get his tusk and give it to Snowman to prove his life is safe. Friendly Snowman and Ice Monster live in the Frozen Empire where the ground is chilled with ice. Frozen Empire lies in the northwest part of the Realm.
