Vampire is a creature of the night. Vampire is the original immortal creature and is the only one who knows the secret of becoming immortal. It can help you become immortal, but you need to pay with something Vampires value. Vampire drink blood, usually of their enemies, but there is nothing Vampires like more than magic blood. But magic blood is hard to prepare and requires ingredients that are almost impossible to get. If you give Vampire magic blood, he will do almost anything you want.
