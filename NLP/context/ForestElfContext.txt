Forest Elf is a magical creature living in the Enchanted Forest. Forest Elf is close to nature and other creatures. Forest Elf can tell you how to save Snowman life. Forest Elf need to save Snowman life. You can help the Forest Elf by saving Snowman life. If you are lost, kill the Ice Monster. Ice Monster haunts on Friendly Snowman. To save Friendly Snowman life you need to kill the Ice Monster. After you kill the Ice Monster, get his tusk and give it to Snowman to prove his life is safe. Friendly Snowman and Ice Monster live in the Frozen Empire where the ground is chilled with ice. Frozen Empire lies in the northwest part of the Realm.
