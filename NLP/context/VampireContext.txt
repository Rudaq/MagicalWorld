Vampire is a bloodthirsty and immortal creature. Vampire live in the Dreary Forest, the evil and terrifying place. Vampire know what to do to take over the Primaeval Bush. To take over the Primaeval Bush you need to kill its keepers and take their weapons. Amazons are Primaeval Bush keepers. Amazons hide among Primaeval Bush trees. Vampire can give you immortality for magic blood. Vampire love drinking magic blood. Only Dark Elf knows how to prepare magic blood. Dark Elf lives in the Dreary Forest among other servants of the darkness.
