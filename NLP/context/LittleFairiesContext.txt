Little Faeries are small creatures with powerful magic that are often overlooked. Little Faeries magic overflows from them in the form of magic dust. Little Faeries have excess magic dust and can give it to those who show they have a pure heart. To show you have a pure heart you need to kill the gorilla and bring its heart. Little Faeries need gorilla heart because it can be a good protection and it can make other enemies withdraw from attacking. Little Faeries are attacked, because they are easy targets for predators. Little Faeries are tasty treats and spiders often hunt them to eat them. Kill the spider and get its fang to protect Little Faeries and in return you will get a magical crystal. Spiders live in the Dreary Forest with other monsters. Little Faeries have lost Flower of Immortality. Dwarf stole Flower of Immortality from Little Faeries. Friendly Snowman lost paper with information about Flower of Immortality. To find Flower of Immortality, find paper and give it to Friendly Snowman. To get the magic dust, you need flame and rocks. Little Faeries can make a magic dust for you. To get the flame, you need to kill Fire Elemental. Fire Elemental is in Desolation of Abomination. To get rocks, you need to kill Earth Elemental. You can find Earth Elemental in Desolation of Abomination.
