Amazons are the ones who know everything about the wilderness. Amazons know all the animals and plants. Amazon can give you any plant you want and show you where to find it. One of the plants that Amazon look after is sage. Amazons can give you sage in exchange for bringing them the skin of the yeti. Amazon want yeti dead, because it scares the animals Amazons take care of and destroys the flora of the forests. The skin of the yeti will help Amazon scare away other predators that can endanger the wilderness and those who live within it.
